Pumped irrigation controller

Clonk's pumped irrigation controller is designed to control irrigation systems with a lower and upper reservoir and two corresponding pumps. It supports flow monitoring on both pumps, up to 13 irrigation stations and tank-level monitoring with up to three float switches per tank.
